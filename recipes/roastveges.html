Oven Roasted Vegetables

Description

An easy, nutritious meal! Simply get your vegetables of choice, cut, oil, and roast!

Ingredients

  • Roast vegetables of choice (recommend 3-4+)
  • Oil
  • Seasoning (all purpose, or a mix)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Cut vegetables into evenly sized chunks so they cook at the same rate
  3. Cover in a small amount of oil for better cooking
  4. Toss in your favourite seasonings. I recommend all purpose.
  5. Chuck in the oven to roast for 30-45 minutes or until cooked through and crispy. Toss frequently.
