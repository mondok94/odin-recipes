Weetbix and Berries

Description

Weetbix, berries and milk! Nothing beats it for brekky.

Ingredients

  • Weetbix
  • Fresh (or frozen) mixed berries of choice
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Place Weetbix in bowl
  2. Add mixed berries
  3. Pour over milk (amount to suit, less for crunchier Weetbix, more for soggier Weetbix!)
  4. Eat! Delicious!
